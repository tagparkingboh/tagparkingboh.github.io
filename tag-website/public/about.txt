# TAG Parking - Bournemouth Airport Meet & Greet Parking

> TAG Parking offers premium meet and greet airport parking at Bournemouth Airport (BOH). We take the stress out of airport parking by collecting your car at the terminal and returning it when you land.

## What We Do

TAG Parking provides a convenient meet and greet service for travellers flying from Bournemouth Airport. Simply drive to the terminal, hand over your keys to our professional driver, and your car is securely parked while you're away. When you return, your car is waiting for you at the terminal.

## Services

- **Meet & Greet Parking**: We meet you at the terminal, park your car securely, and return it when you land
- **Secure Storage**: Your vehicle is kept in a secure, monitored compound
- **Flexible Booking**: Book online 24/7, modify or cancel easily
- **No Bus Transfers**: Unlike traditional parking, you're dropped and picked up right at the terminal

## How It Works

1. **Book Online**: Reserve your parking at tagparking.co.uk
2. **Drop Off**: Drive to Bournemouth Airport terminal, meet our driver at the designated area
3. **Hand Over Keys**: Our driver takes your car to secure storage
4. **Fly**: Enjoy your trip without parking worries
5. **Return**: When you land, your car is brought back to the terminal
6. **Drive Home**: Collect your keys and you're on your way
